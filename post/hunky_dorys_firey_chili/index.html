Hunky Dorys - Firey Chilli

Posted on Oct 15, 2016

Hunky Dorys - Firey Chilli

On a mundane trip to spar on an uneventful Tuesday evening, a new Hunky Dory flavour was discovered that neither of us had tried. The initial excitement was short lived as ** tasted the first crisp. A bland taste followed by a bust of “Fiery” Flames. The crisps taste rather oily at the start and there is no specific flavour, such as paprika, sour cream or salt to them. You are then hit by the spiciness which makes it impossible to really enjoy the crisps as your taste buds will quickly become numb. Usually Hunky Dorys convince with their rich flavours, thus it was a disappointment to realise that Hunky Dorys didn’t display their usual pedigree.

Hopefully they will shine brighter with their next try.

Conclusions: 2 of 5 Stars
